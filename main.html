Plot Summary

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction thriller that delves into the world of dreams. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who can infiltrate the subconscious, is given a chance to have his criminal record erased by performing an inception: planting an idea into someone's mind. As the mission unfolds, the boundaries between reality and dreams blur, leading to a gripping and complex narrative.

Actors

The movie features a stellar cast including Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito.
